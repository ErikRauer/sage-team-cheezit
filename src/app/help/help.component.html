Rules of SAGE
  • SAGE is played with two or more people, with one person being the host and the other(s) playing the game.
  • The point of the game is for the player(s) to guess what the definition of a word is on a card.
  • Each word has four hints: a synonym, an antonymn, a general sense and an example usage. A player may use a hint to help them guess the definition by pressing the hint button on the card, but the hints are random.
  • Each card starts with 5 points. Each time a hint is used, a point is deducted.
  • If the player(s) don't guess the definition correctly, move on to the next card. If they guess correctly, press the "Got it!" button.
  • Have fun!
    • Decks and Cards
  • Once you are logged in with your Google Account, you may create decks and add cards to that deck.
  • To view your decks, navigate to "My Decks" on the Home Page or the Sidebar. There are also Public Decks located on the "Public Decks" page.
  • To create a deck, click the + icon on the bottom right corner of the page and give the deck a name. Click add deck and you will see it appear in the deck list.
  • To add cards to a deck, click on the deck's title. Click the + icon on the bottom right corner of the page and add a word, synonym(s), antonym(s), general sense(s) and example Usage(s).
  • You may edit/delete cards in the deck by clicking the respective icons in the card view.
    • Classes
  • You may create a class(s) so multiple users have access to decks.
  • To create a class, open the Sidebar by clicking the menu icon in the upper left corner. Next to "Classes" click the + icon and give your class a name.
  • To join a class, have the teacher open their class view and scan the QR code or copy the link.
  • To add decks, click the + icon in the lower right corner and give it name and decide if it should be shared or not.
  • Shared decks allow students in the class to add to the deck. If the student created the card, they may also edit/delete it.
  • To add a card to a deck, click the + icon on the bottom right corner of the page and add a word, synonym(s), antonym(s), general sense(s) and example Usage(s).
    • Multiplayer
  • SAGE can be played with multiple devices. The owner of the deck/classroom is the host and everyone else is a player.
  • To start a multiplayer game, either go to "My Decks" or a classroom that you manage. Click "Play" on the deck.
  • To join a game, go to "Join a Game" and enter the join code as displayed on the host's screen.
  • The host controls the "Hint?" and "Got It!" buttons.
